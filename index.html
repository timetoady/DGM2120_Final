Lolo

Gem of the Big Sky

Have you ever wanted to take a vacation... from your vacation?

Are you the type? You scrimp and save your vacation days, and finally get a couple weeks off, only to spend it rushing from museum to restaurant to site-seeing point, cramming activities in every hour of the day.

What if you could go some place and just... relax? Enjoy fresh air, clean water, inexpensive food, and nature's bounty less than your daily commute away in almost every direction...

It's so beautiful. Skies just like you'd expect from Montana, great food (no sales tax!), so many nice places to hike, and all the peace and quiet to read a book you could ask for.
― Mary, avid reader.
It's a tiny paradise. The humidity is perfect, the water taists amazing, a place to ski an hour's drive away, and a hot spring just up this pretty little highway. Love jumping back and forth between the hot and cold pools.
― Tim, three-time returner.
It's cool because food is cheap, but really it's a driver's paradise. They have miles and miles of empty, winding highway, fast speeds, gorgeous landscapes... Not a better place to be on the road, whether you come on four wheels or two.
― Alex, motorhead.

Ready for freedom and relaxation?
