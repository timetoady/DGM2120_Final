Packages

Hot Spring Gettaway

Enjoy four days and three nights deep in the heart of Montana at Lolo Hot Springs. Stay at The Lodge at Lolo Hot Springs. Enjoy a relaxing soak in the hot springs, take a nice winter hike, sledding, horseback riding, or just settle up for a nice book. Within easy drive of resteraunts and grocery. Includes car rental through Enterprise.

Total cost of the entire package: $550! (includes tax!)

Lakeside

Four days and three nights in a luxury AirBnB suite on the lake. Enjoy fishing, swimming, boating, and easy access to downtown Lolo. Includes car rental through Enterprise.

Total cost of the entire package: $500! (includes tax!)

Lazy River

Three days and two nights in the both rustic and luxurious Blue Mountain Lodge, just a short drive from the Bitterroot River. Enjoy a lazy float down the river, fishing, canooing, and ready access to both Missoula and Lolo stores, shops, and restaurants. Includes car rental through Enterprise.

Total cost of the entire package: $500! (includes tax!)
